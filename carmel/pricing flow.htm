Ratebook Pricing Flow

The pricing flow works in layers: start from the base zone-to-zone list price, then apply class-based adjustments, then add time-based adjustments when applicable.

Core formula
Final Price = List Price + List Price Adj + Base Price + Base Price LOC Adj + Base Price Time Adj + Base Price Time Lvl Adj

Pricing flow at a glance

1

List Price

The starting price. It is based on the PU Zone and DO Zone.

Step-by-step explanation

Step 1

List Price

The List Price is the starting price. It is based on the Car Class 'SD', the PU Zone, and the DO Zone.

zone 1 area zone 1 name zone 2 area zone 2 name list price
M M0 M M0 17
M M0 M M2 17
⇋ Bi-Directional, unless a specific alternative rule exists.
Step 2

List Price Adjust

Does not apply to M→M.

Adjusts the price on specific Zones depending on PU and DO location.

zone 1 area zone 1 name zone 2 area zone 2 name list price adjust
SI SI M M3 2
SI SI M M4 3
M M0 BX BX 10
M M2 BX BX 9

Example: Trips from BX to Downtown Manhattan require an additional charge compared to BX to Upper Manhattan, and the same adjustment applies in the reverse direction.

⇒ Directional. If the reverse direction is not defined, the adjustment defaults to 0.
Step 3

Base Price

Adjusts the price based on the Car Class and wether the trip is one-way or round-trip.

car class adjust factor adjust amt rt adjust factor rt adjust amt
DX 1 8 2 12
SD 1 2
SX 2 12 4 24
VN 2 4

Pricing behavior:

  • One-way: List Price × Adjust Factor + Adjust Amt
  • Round trip: List Price × Rt Adjust Factor + Rt Adjust Amt
⇋ Bi-Directional, unless a specific alternative rule exists.
Step 4

Base Price LOC Adjust

Adjusts the prices based on longer distance trips within specific Zones.

zone 1 name zone 2 name car class adjust factor adjust amt
SI BO LX 1 10
BK OT DX 1 5
BX M DX 1 6
QU QURK DX 1 5

QURK = Far away Rockaway

⇒ Directional. If the reverse direction is not defined, the adjustment defaults to 0.
Step 5

Base Price Time Adj

Adjusts pricing when a trip falls into unique/special Time Slices.

pu loc do loc car class time slice adjust factor adjust amt
MN BO LX AM Rush From NYC 1 2
MN BO SD CHRISTMAS AM 1 5
MN BO SV THANKSGIVING PM 1 10

These settings are found under Settings > Dispatch Settings > Time Slice Setup

⇒ Directional. If the reverse direction is not defined, the adjustment defaults to 0.
Step 6

Base Price Time Lvl Adj

Adjusts the price based on certain Time Slices within specific Zones.

Time Slices are defined in Ratebook > Time Slice Setup
⇒ Directional. If the reverse direction is not defined, the adjustment defaults to 0.

All Borroughs

Adjusts the price according to the Time Slice Level amount.

Defined in Peak (Rush) Adjustments under Settings > Dispatch Settings

zone 1 name zone 2 name car class time slice time slice level
BO BO DX PEAK MORNING BORO C
BO OT DX PEAK MORNING BORO C
M BO VN Afternoon Peak L
EWR BO DX PEAK MORNING EWR C

Local

Defined in Local (Non Rush) Special under Settings > Dispatch Settings

Applies to the Car Classes SD SO LX DS

zone 1 name zone 2 name car class time slice adjust factor adjust amt
M M DX AM LOCALS SPECIAL 1 3
M M SO AM LOCALS SPECIAL 1 3
Peak Codes (N, L, H, C, K) do NOT apply to locals.
